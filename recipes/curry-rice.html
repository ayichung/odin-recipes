Curry Rice

Description

Ingredients
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 potato
  • 1/2 onion
  • 3/4 lb beef
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 4 curry roux cubes
Steps
  1. Cut carrot, potato, onion, and beef into roughly 1-inch cubes.
  2. Add oil to a pot and stir-fry cut ingredients for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add water to pot on high heat until it boils.
  4. Simmer for about 10-15 minutes on medium heat until ingredients are fully cooked.
  5. Add curry roux cubes and simmer for about 5 more minutes.
  6. Serve over white rice.
