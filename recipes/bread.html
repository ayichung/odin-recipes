Bread

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cup warm water
  • 2 1/4 tsp instant yeast
  • 1/2 tbsp table salt

Steps

  1. Mix water, yeast, salt, and flour in a bowl.
  2. Cover and let rise for 2 hours until doubled in size.
  3. Refrigerate overnight for about 12-16 hours.
  4. The next day, place dutch oven with lid in oven and preheat to 450 F.
  5. Let dough sit at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  6. Scrape dough from bowl onto floured surface and fold about six times to center.
  7. Flour parchment paper and flip dough onto paper, then score and flour top.
  8. Remove dutch oven from oven and place dough with parchment paper inside.
  9. Bake for 35 minutes with the lid on and 15 minutes with the lid off.
