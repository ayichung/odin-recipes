Tomato Pasta

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 oz dry pasta
  • 1/3 cup tomato pasta sauce
  • 1 handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 cup meat or seafood

Steps

  1. Cook meat or seafood in a pan with oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Boil water with a pinch of salt in a pot, then add pasta and cook for 11 minutes.
  3. Drain pasta in a coriander.
  4. Return pot to heat and add tomato pasta sauce to simmer.
  5. Add pasta and meat or seafood to a dish and pour tomato pasta sauce over top.
  6. Slice basil and add on top.
